La Pavoni Gran Caffè

La Pavoni, making quality espresso machines since 1905, is world-renowned for its exquisite espresso and cappuccino makers.

La Pavoni Gran Caffè is the perfect choice for a home espresso machine, complete with a brass group, porterfilter and boiler to obtain a better thermal stability. The Turbo attachment simplifies the traditional way of frothing milk.

  • Group, 53 mm porterfilter and boiler in brass
  • Coffee pod adaptor
  • Pressure gauge for measuring accurate extraction of espresso (GCM only)
  • Features an ultra quiet ULKA pump
  • Turbo frother attachment
  • On/off switches (GCN) and lever type switches (GCM)
  • Body made of metal (GCN) and polished stainless steel (GCM)
