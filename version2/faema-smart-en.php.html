Faema Smart

The new Smart has been completely restyled by Giugiaro Design, giving the machine a totally new look in keeping with the latest contemporary design.  With eye-catching forms emphasizing its young, vibrant character and sophisticated colours, the Smart is perfectly suited to all interior decor styles.  The new control panel has been designed for improved ergonomics and reliability.  

The Smart is only available in 2 groups Automatic version where coffee is delivered according to previously programmed doses.

  • Automatic boiler fill
  • Double gauge to control pump and boiler pressure
  • Four beverage selections with programmable shot dosing and one continuous delivery/stop push button per group
