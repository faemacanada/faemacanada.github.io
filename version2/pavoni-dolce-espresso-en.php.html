La Pavoni Dolce Espresso PID

La Pavoni, making quality espresso machines since 1905, is world-renowned for its exquisite espresso and cappuccino makers.

La Pavoni Dolce Espresso PID is the perfect choice for a home espresso machine, complete with a brass group, porterfilter and boiler to obtain a better thermal stability.  Equipped with PID, the user can adjust the temperature of the coffee in a range from 80°C to 100°C.  The Turbo attachment simplifies the traditional way of frothing milk.

  • Group, 53 mm porterfilter and boiler in brass
  • Coffee pod adaptor
  • Equipped with PID which allows the user to adjust the temperature of the coffee in a range from 80°C to 100°C. 
  • Features an ultra quiet ULKA pump
  • Turbo frother attachment
  • Lever type switches
  • Body made of polished stainless steel
