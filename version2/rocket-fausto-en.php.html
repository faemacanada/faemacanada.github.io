Rocket Fausto

The Rocket Fausto grinder brings all of the features and functionality professional baristas love to the kitchen, but in a more compact way.

Equipped with commercial quality 65mm flat burrs, Fausto grinds quickly and accurately with easy to dial in stepless grinding.  The digital display with shot counter and programmable dual control buttons allows for quick adjustments and effortless portion control.

  • Stepless micrometrical grinding adjustment
  • On-demand grinding with electronic dose adjustment
  • Single and double dose with independent adjustment
  • Digital display with shot counter
  • Push button panel operating device
  • Manual continuous grinding
