PPR Single Grill

With today's high demand for panini sandwiches made popular in Italy, the PPR panini grill by Sirman is a must for most restaurants and cafés.  The panini grill can also be used for many other purposes: grilling meats, fish, vegetables etc.

 

  • Plates are made of a special cast iron covered by a vitreous enamel coating
  • Completely ribbed, top and bottom
  • Stainless steel construction
  • Shock-proof heating elements mounted inside the plates for perfect thermal conductivity
  • Adjustable thermostat up to 300° C
  • Self-balancing upper plate adjusts to different heights
