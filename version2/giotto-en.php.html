Rocket Giotto Chronometer Type V

Rocket Giotto Chronometer Evoluzione R

Some people consider this handmade espresso machine, the Giotto by Rocket Espresso, as legendary.  It is beautifully made in stainless steel with meticulous care and attention to detail. Featuring the infamous E61 group head, you can now brew a perfect espresso in your own home as one made by your favourite barista.  Available in 2 versions: Type V, with PID and a vibration type pump, in pour-over version and the Evoluzione R, with PID and a full commercial rotary type pump, which, by a simple flip of a switch, can work either pour-over or direct water connection.

  • All stainless steel body
  • E61 commercial group head
  • Digital shot timer allowing the user to better optimise extraction time
  • Dual pre-infusion system: mechanical pre-infusion system with progressively working piston and static pre-infusion chamber. This reflects into smooth coffee extraction with optimum aroma, body and ''crema''.
  • Superior layout of thermosyphon system resulting in optimum extraction with a 20% higher volume as with comparable machines on the market
  • Boiler is made of pure copper (Cu. 99.9%) with two lead free, heavy brass end plates.  These brass end plates combined with the special additional boiler insulation increase the thermal retention by up to 50% versus other boiler systems.  
  • One dedicated gauge for pump pressure and one for boiler pressure control
  • Stainless steel steam wand with patented cool-touch technology: anti-burn (only the nozzle gets hot); easy cleaning as milk doesn't bake on wand; more efficient steam, as heat is not dissipated around the wand.  Steam nozzle designed for easy milk foaming with fine texture.
  • Microprocessor controlled automatic level control of boiler and fresh water reservoir, with low water level indication system
  • Both versions use PID control for greater temperature stability drawing on 4 signal parameters to measure and control the temperature.  A digital and adjustable temperature display allows adjustable brewing temperatures for different coffee types.
  • Vibration type pump for Type V and full commercial rotary type pump for Evoluzione R
  • Pour-over for Type V; pour-over or alternatively by turning the control switch, steel braided direct water connection (hard plumbed) with full drip tray drainage system for Evoluzione R
  • Optimized boiler capacity to thermosyphon system ratio.  Results in fast heat up time with 1200 watt element, made from special long life material (5 times longer than normal elements)
  • Fully certified commercially rated safety valve
  • Comes with stainless steel cup rail, 2 full size commercial porterfilters (a single and a double), a blind filter, a metal tamper and adjustable feet
