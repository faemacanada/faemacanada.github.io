Marzotto Caffè Beans

The Marzotto Lusso coffee is a blend of high quality Arabica coffee beans with a share of 20% Robusta, which is responsible for producing an excellent crema.  Its flavor and aroma is wonderfully broad with deep flavors on the palate followed by a slightly nutty chocolate after taste.

Available in a 1 kg bag.
