La Pavoni Jolly

La Pavoni Jolly coffee grinder brings the aroma of freshly ground coffee into your home by making espresso and cappuccino like those found at an authentic Italian coffee bar.  The coffee grinder has a coffee bean hopper of 250 gram capacity and enables a wide coffee grind adjustment.  The container for the ground coffee can be taken out for easy cleaning (JL only).  This grinder is fitted with a dosing device (JDL and JDR only) supplying the correct quantity of ground coffee for one cup.

La Pavoni base BL features a coffee grounds drawer with filter bumper and fits all lever and pump driven machines with grinder.

  • Burr grinder
  • Doserless grinder (JL) and doser grinder (JDL and JDR)
  • Option to select different types of grinds
