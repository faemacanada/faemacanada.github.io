Pola

Create delicious, homemade ice cream using this commercial quality machine, which produces up to 2 litres of ice cream in less than 20 minutes.  The Pola ice cream machine by Musso is a completely self-contained freezing unit made in Italy.  The housing, including bowl and blade, are made of stainless steel.  Operation is simplified by the use of a timer which works in conjunction with a churn and chill switch.

  • Commercial batch ice cream machine
  • Ideal for ice cream, sorbets, frozen yogurts etc.
  • Self-contained compressor which makes ice cream in 20 minutes
  • No need for additional cooling solutions (such as brine or water and salt mixtures)
  • Non-removable stainless steel bowl with stainless steel blade
  • See-through lid lets you control every stage of ice cream making
  • All stainless steel housing
