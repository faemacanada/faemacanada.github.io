The Faema Team

 

Faema Distributeur Inc. has been established since 1968 with its main office located at 14 Jean Talon West in Montreal and is the exclusive importer and distributor of Faema espresso and cappuccino machines. 

The company was founded by Graziano Angeli, an immigrant from Italy, who wanted to teach Canadians what a real coffee tasted like. It took many years of preaching but finally people have come to realize the importance of a good cup of coffee, hence the increase in popularity of specialty coffees over the years. 

Today, along with Faema products, we have added to our line other Italian products such as espresso coffee beans, pasta machines, panini grills, ice cream machines etc.

 

Come pay us a visit at our newly renovated store at 14 Jean Talon West corner of                              St. Laurent.  Looking forward to seeing you there !
