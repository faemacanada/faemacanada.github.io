La Pavoni Stradivari

La Pavoni, making quality espresso machines since 1905, is world-renowned for its exquisite espresso and cappuccino makers.

The Stradivari was designed by drawing inspiration from the violin, a work of art thanks to the famous lute-maker, Antonio Stradivari. The base resembles the violin case and the lever, the bow.  By lifting the lever, one raises a piston inside the group, allowing water under pressure to infuse the coffee in the filter holder.  The lever is then lowered causing the piston to force the water through the coffee into the cup.  The user can therefore vary the speed at which the water passes through the coffee ensuring it is made exactly to his requirements.

 

 

  • 0.8 liter (20 oz.) boiler capacity, capable of making 8 - 2 oz. cups of espresso for STL model
  • 1.6 liter (38 oz.) boiler capacity, capable of making 16 - 2 oz. cups of espresso for SPL and SGR models
  • Smooth piston action makes brewing espresso easy and fun
  • Includes Cappuccino Automatic milk frothing system and traditional manual milk frother
  • Safety valve on top of the boiler
  • Monitor steam pressure with the mounted pressure gauge for SPL and SGR models only
  • Water level gauge which indicates how much water is remaining in the boiler
  • Easy-to-clean drip tray
  • Internal thermostat monitors brewing temperature with precision
  • Long lasting stainless steel heating elements
  • Makes one or two cups at a time
